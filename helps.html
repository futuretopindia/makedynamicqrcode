You can download your final QR code in multiple high-quality formats:

A dynamic QR code allows you to change the destination URL or content *after* the QR code has been printed. Our "Multi-URL" feature is a type of dynamic QR code. In contrast, most of our other types (like a standard URL) are "static," meaning the content is fixed once the code is generated.

Frames add a stylish border and a "call to action" (like "SCAN ME") around your QR code. Simply go to the "Frame" card, select a style you like (e.g., Circle, Ribbon, Envelope), and type your desired text. You can even customize the frame and text colors! When you download as PNG/JPEG, the frame is included.
